Research interests
  • Probability theory. My current projects involve problems from statistical physics. More precisely, I am interested in studying
    1. critical or near-critical behavior in random spatial models (such as Bernoulli percolation and many of its variants), and
    2. random optimization problems that appear in complex or disordered systems (e.g. first-passage percolation, spin glasses), and their applications.
